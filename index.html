About Us

At Sane Hut, our collective vision is to keep the world sane. And huts, here denotes a traditional setting for African homes. Metaphorically, our platform which would be a social network, is considered home where people across the world with mental disorders would find sanity. Clearly, the world was not prepared for the COVID-19 pandemic that hit us despite being forewarned, and it comes as a regret. But come to think of it, have we learnt our lessons? Is the world prepared for the illness that will affect one in every four people at the some point of their lives? Is the world prepared for what is expected to be the lead in causes of ill-health and disability worldeide by 2030? How are we even managing the four hundred and fifty (450) million existing cases? Luckily for us, treatments are available, but nearly tow thirds of people with know mental health disorders never seek help from a health professional, according to the World Health Organization. Among the core reasons that prevent health care and treatment from reaching people with mental disorders are stigma, discrimination, and neglect; at the institional level, the lack of urgency, misinformation, and competing demands are blinding policy-makers from taking stock of a situation where mental disorders figure among the leading causes of diseases and disability in the world, says the World Health Organization. The Sane Hut project has streamlined depression, a leading mental disorder (also a leading cause of global suicide cases) affecting over two hundred and sixty four million (264 million) people of all ages as its main focus while tackling these underlying problems related with mental health disorders.

How sanehut Works

The Sane Hut protect seeks to provide solution or minimize to the following Problems close the globe: 1.The problem of accessibility to mental health care 2. The problems of stigmatization, discrimination, and negligence to seek mental health care, 3.The problem of inadequate data to support healthcare decisions by stakeholders and policy makers. Below are the solutions or the treatment approaches Sane Hut would employ to tackle the above challenges: 1. Developing an application software that would detect common symptoms (particularly, emotional statuses) of depression with Machine learning algorithms. a. The product would be a social network connecting users with symptoms (targeting minor symptoms) to psychotherapists or health professions for social or health care through live interactions. b. The product would be data-driven to allow keeping “stocks” of cases and making them available for policy making.
